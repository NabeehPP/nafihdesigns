Our Process

Four layers of clarity

Strategic branding happens through structured methodology. Each phase builds on discovery to create identities rooted in insight.

01 Discovery

Competitive mapping, stakeholder interviews, and market research reveal where your brand should land and why.

02 Strategy

Positioning, tone, visual direction, and naming architecture are defined before a single mark is drawn.

03 Design

Purposeful systems built with typography, color, mark, and motion working cohesively together.

04 Delivery

Complete guidelines, production-ready files, and a launch toolkit that your team can confidently execute.

About Nafih

The studio

Nafih Design is a premium brand identity studio founded on the belief that great design is rooted in strategy. We work with ambitious brands—from funded startups to established market leaders—across industries and geographies.

Our strength lies in synthesis: combining strategic thinking with visual craft to create marks that feel inevitable once you see them. Every project is approached as a long-term partnership.

Philosophy

We don't design logos. We design positioning made visible.

Approach

Research, strategy, design, delivery. No shortcuts.

Timeline
